Project Overview

Booking Platform for Premium Clients

Limousine London is a high-end chauffeur service operating across Greater London, catering to corporate executives, VIP clients, and airport transfers. They needed a booking experience as premium as their vehicles.

WelkinGetter delivered a custom booking platform where clients can check real-time vehicle availability, select service packages, pay securely via Stripe, and instantly receive SMS confirmations — with a fully branded, luxury aesthetic that converts hesitant visitors into confirmed bookings.

Client
Limousine London — a premium chauffeur company serving corporate and private clients across the UK capital with a fleet of luxury vehicles.
The Challenge

What We Were Solving

Limousine London was taking bookings via phone calls and WhatsApp — a system prone to double-bookings and revenue leakage. Key pain points:

Manual booking led to frequent scheduling conflicts
No online payment — clients had to call or bank transfer
No automated reminders — high no-show rate
Existing website felt generic — didn't reflect luxury brand
Key Goals

What Success Looked Like

Self-service online booking with real-time availability
Stripe payment processing with instant confirmation
Automated SMS reminders 24h before each booking
Premium dark-luxury design to match brand positioning
Our Solution

How WelkinGetter Engineered It

We built a custom booking engine on WooCommerce with a real-time availability calendar synced to Google Calendar, Stripe for secure card payments, and Twilio for automated SMS confirmations and reminders.

Real-Time Availability

Live calendar synced to Google Calendar — no double-bookings, ever.

Stripe Payments

Secure card processing with instant booking confirmation emails and receipts.

SMS Automation

Twilio-powered SMS confirmations and 24-hour reminders — zero no-shows.

Luxury UI Design

Dark, premium aesthetic with gold accents — brand-aligned and conversion-optimised.
