Project Overview

Digital Presence Built on Trust & Security

Suveinakama Legal is a boutique law firm specialising in corporate, property, and immigration law. Their clients needed to share sensitive documents digitally — but without the security risks of email attachments.

WelkinGetter delivered a secure, professional website with an encrypted client portal for document submission, integrated Calendly-style appointment scheduling, a case updates tracker, and a clean legal aesthetic that inspires confidence from the first visit.

The Challenge

What We Were Solving

Legal professionals handle some of the most sensitive documents imaginable. The firm's existing digital workflow had critical gaps:

Clients emailing sensitive legal documents — major security risk
Appointments booked by phone — staff spending 2+ hrs/day on scheduling
No website trust signals — potential clients had no way to verify credibility
Clients couldn't track their case status — constant phone enquiries
Key Goals

What Success Looked Like

Encrypted document upload portal for clients
Self-service appointment booking (no phone calls)
Professional, trust-inducing website design
Case status portal to reduce inbound calls
Our Solution

How WelkinGetter Engineered It

We built a security-first legal website on WordPress with a custom AES-256 encrypted document portal, Calendly scheduling integration, and a restricted client login area for case status updates.

Encrypted Document Portal

AES-256 encrypted file uploads — clients submit documents safely, lawyers access securely.

Appointment Scheduling

Integrated Calendly with consultation types, duration options, and auto-confirmation emails.

Case Status Tracker

Password-protected client portal showing real-time case milestones and document status.

Trust-First Design

Professional navy/white aesthetic with credential display, team profiles, and client testimonials.
