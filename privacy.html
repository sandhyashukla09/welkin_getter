Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026  |  Effective Date: April 23, 2026

This policy is prepared in compliance with India's Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023 and applicable provisions of the Information Technology Act, 2000. We are committed to handling your data responsibly and transparently.

1. Who We Are

WelkinGetter Global IT Solutions ("WelkinGetter", "we", "us", or "our") is a technology services company registered in India, headquartered in Indore, Madhya Pradesh. We provide custom web development, LMS platforms, mobile applications, and digital marketing services.

Contact: hello@welkingetter.com  |  +91 97703 29202

2. Data We Collect

We collect personal data only when you provide it voluntarily or when it is necessary to deliver our services:

  • Contact information: Name, email address, phone number (via contact and enquiry forms)
  • Business information: Company name, job title, project requirements
  • Newsletter subscription: Email address only
  • Usage data: Pages visited, time on site, browser type (via cookies — see Section 7)
  • Communications: Emails and messages you send us

We do not collect sensitive personal data (e.g. financial details, health data, government IDs) through our website.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To respond to your enquiries and schedule consultations
  • To deliver agreed services and send project-related communications
  • To send our newsletter (only if you have subscribed)
  • To improve our website and understand visitor behaviour (aggregated, anonymised)
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Under the DPDP Act 2023, we process your personal data on the following bases:

  • Consent: Where you have given explicit consent (e.g. newsletter sign-up, enquiry forms)
  • Contractual necessity: To fulfil or prepare for a service contract with you
  • Legitimate interests: For site analytics and improving our services, where your rights are not overridden
  • Legal obligation: Where required by Indian law

5. Data Sharing

We may share your data with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our business, subject to strict confidentiality agreements:

  • Email service providers (for transactional and newsletter emails)
  • Web analytics tools (anonymised data only)
  • Cloud infrastructure providers (data stored in India or EU-adequate regions)

We will disclose personal data if required by Indian courts, law enforcement, or regulatory authorities.

6. Data Retention

  • Enquiry / contact data: Retained for up to 3 years after last contact, unless a project commences
  • Client project data: Retained for 7 years to comply with tax and contractual obligations
  • Newsletter data: Retained until you unsubscribe
  • Website analytics: Aggregated and anonymised; retained indefinitely

7. Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies only (session management, security). We do not use third-party advertising cookies. You may disable cookies in your browser settings; essential site functions will remain available.

8. Your Rights (DPDP Act 2023)

As a Data Principal under the DPDP Act 2023, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data
  • Withdraw consent at any time (without affecting prior lawful processing)
  • Erasure of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements
  • Nominate a person to exercise rights on your behalf
  • File a grievance with our Data Protection Officer

To exercise any right, email us at hello@welkingetter.com with the subject line "Data Rights Request". We will respond within 30 days.

9. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including AES-256 encryption for data in transit and at rest, restricted access controls, and regular security reviews. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure; we encourage you to contact us via secure channels for sensitive communications.

10. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are directed to business professionals. We do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under 18 years of age. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated via email (if you are a subscriber or client) or a prominent notice on our website. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
