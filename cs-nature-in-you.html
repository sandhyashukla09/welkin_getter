Project Overview

One Codebase. Two Platforms. Zero Compromise.

Nature In You is a wellness and natural lifestyle brand offering organic products, guided routines, and expert content. They needed a mobile app that felt premium on both iOS and Android — without paying for two separate development teams.

WelkinGetter delivered a single Flutter codebase that renders pixel-perfectly on both platforms: a curated product catalogue with category filtering, personalised push notifications, a guided wellness content library, and a frictionless one-tap checkout flow with Razorpay integration.

The app launched on both stores simultaneously — on time, on budget, and with a 4.7-star average user rating within the first month.

The Challenge

What We Were Solving

The brand had a decent website but zero mobile presence. Their target audience — health-conscious millennials — were primarily mobile users. The core challenges:

No mobile app — 70% of traffic was mobile but conversion was low
Budget for one development team — needed iOS and Android both
No way to send personalised product notifications to users
Checkout was 7 steps — cart abandonment was above 65%
Key Goals

What Success Looked Like

Single Flutter app for iOS + Android — one team, one codebase
Personalised push notifications via Firebase Cloud Messaging
Streamlined 2-tap checkout with Razorpay UPI + cards
Wellness content library with video and article support
Simultaneous launch on both app stores within 14 weeks
Our Solution

How WelkinGetter Built It

We used Flutter for a single high-performance codebase across both platforms, Firebase for auth and real-time database, Firebase Cloud Messaging for smart push notifications, and Razorpay for seamless Indian payment flows.

Flutter Cross-Platform

One codebase, two stores — identical premium experience on iOS and Android with native performance.

Smart Push Notifications

Firebase Cloud Messaging sends personalised product alerts, wellness reminders, and offer notifications.

2-Tap Checkout

Razorpay integration with UPI, cards, and wallets — cart to payment confirmed in under 30 seconds.

Wellness Content Library

In-app articles and video routines curated by category — keeps users engaged between purchases.
